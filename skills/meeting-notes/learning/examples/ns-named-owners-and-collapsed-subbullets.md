Source: S&S Segmentation Brainstorm Internal 2.19.26 (Session 2)

## Pattern 1: Named individual owners replace "Team to"

**Before (AI):**
- **Team to** send a priming note to S&S ahead of the meeting
  - Include prompts for S&S to think through beforehand; surface ingoing objections (e.g., on vertical) early so the team can prepare stronger evidence

**After (User):**
- **Kevin to** send a priming note to S&S ahead of the meeting including prompts to guide thinking

**Lesson:** When a specific Seurat team member owns an action, use their first name bold as the bullet owner. Fold essential detail into the main line and cut the sub-bullet.

## Pattern 2: Survey actions split by owner, not grouped under "Team to"

**Before (AI):**
- **Team to** execute survey sample-building plan
  - Send marketing blast, follow-up emails (unique links), and website popup early next week
  - Identify 1–2 targeted LinkedIn groups; reach out to Adam/PPAI about a last call next Tuesday...

**After (User):**

- **Kyra/Kayla to** research LinkedIn ads and identify 1–2 targeted LinkedIn groups

**Lesson:** When sub-bullets have distinct owners, promote them to separate main bullets with named ownership. Keep the "Team to" bullet for the collective action, stripped of the items that now have owners.
