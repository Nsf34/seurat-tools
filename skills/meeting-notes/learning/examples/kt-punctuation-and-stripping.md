# Example: Key Takeaways — Punctuation & Stripping

Source: Henkel Category Growth Vision Regroup External 2.6.26 (Session 1)

---

## Semicolons not em dashes

**Before (AI):**
Carlos aligned that the **updated demand space names are directionally stronger** — Elevated Expression in particular resonated as a strong fit for Got2b's equity and self-expression alignment

**After (User):**
Carlos aligned that the **updated demand space names are directionally stronger**; Elevated Expression in particular resonated as a strong fit for Got2b's equity and self-expression alignment

**Lesson:** Use semicolons, not em dashes, to join two clauses within a Key Takeaway bullet.

---

## Parentheses not em dashes for inline lists

**Before (AI):**
Mark flagged that **three demand spaces have no Henkel brand coverage** — Out the Door, Keep it Clean, Me Moment — and expects this could spark "rich conversation" with the broader team

**After (User):**
Mark flagged that **three demand spaces have no Henkel brand coverage** (Out the Door, Keep it Clean, Me Moment) and expects this could spark "rich conversation" with the broader team

**Lesson:** Use parentheses to set off inline lists or examples mid-bullet; do not surround with em dashes.

---

## Strip forward-looking clauses

**Before (AI):**
Carlos flagged that the distinction between **Hair Health Haven (dedicated deep dive) and Healthy Habits (quick routine)** must be clearly articulated for the broader team — will be critical ahead of the Linda conversation

**After (User):**
Carlos flagged that the distinction between **Hair Health Haven (dedicated deep dive) and Healthy Habits (quick routine)** must be clearly articulated for the broader team

**Lesson:** Key Takeaway bullets state the fact; cut trailing implications or forward-looking context.

---

## Strip attribution and opinion; state the fact

**Before (AI — two bullets):**
- Mark wants **"evangelical fervor" from Linda** — not just her acceptance — and stressed her specific concerns must be addressed head-on in both the deck and the discussion
- Mindy surfaced that **Linda has a negative association with "salon quality,"** possibly tied to pro side sensitivity; understanding and addressing this is important before the Feb. 17 alignment

**After (User — one bullet):**
- **Linda has a negative association with "salon quality,"** possibly tied to pro side sensitivity

**Lesson:** Remove attribution ("Mindy surfaced that..."), remove action implications ("understanding this is important before..."), and remove bullets expressing what someone *wants*. Consolidate to the core fact. Opinion/desire language belongs in Full Notes.
